About Page

We live in an world where music is very popular across the world. Music has become a way of uplifting people form depression or a tough day. It is a tool of bringing people together when celebrating parties and motivating those who workout. It is used as a way to calm the mind and help people relax. Some people with a passion in music want to be able to play and learn a certain instrument. The motive for building this site is to provide a brief explanation of how instruments are played and how they work. This site also has a page where the user can purchase an instrument from another site that they have an interest in. More resources are provided with links to more in depth page of the certain family of instrument.
